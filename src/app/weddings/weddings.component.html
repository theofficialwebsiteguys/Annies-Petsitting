Let's include your animal babies in your big day!

Personalized wedding plans for your Dogs, Cats, or any other pet!

  • Meet with Bride and Groom and with Venue and Vendors
  • Attend Rehearsal with pets, bring the animal(s) to the wedding venue, pictures, and reception
  • Take care of pet's needs during the event (bathroom, water, feeding, treats, dressing pet in needed clothing/accessories)
  • Help pose pets for photos
  • Handles pet, supervises pets, and takes pets on walks around venue
  • Petsitting at your home/hotel while you're at the wedding (visits, overnights)
  • Stay with your pets while you're on your honeymoon
